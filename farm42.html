FARM42

Application, Branding, UX/UI Design, Service Design

2022

Overview

Web application for precision farming, helping farmers manage fields, zones, irrigation, and crops efficiently. Users can upload or draw fields on maps, access weather forecasts, and work on both desktop and touch devices. Designed for usability in challenging conditions, including tractors and outdoor environments.

My Role

Design

UX/UI and web application design from early concept to final implementation.

Branding

Created visual identity and interface styling for a consistent, user-friendly experience.

Collaboration

Worked with developers and service designers to integrate features and maintain usability across devices.

Problem-Solving

Designed intuitive tools for managing fields, zones, irrigation, crops, and integrating third-party weather data.

Tools & Technologies

Figma Illustrator InVision Responsive web design

Key Features

Field Management

Create, edit, and upload field maps (.shp, .kml, GeoTIFF) or draw fields freely.

Irrigation & Crop Management

Assign irrigation zones, monitor crops, and schedule activities.

Weather & Forecast Integration

Display third-party weather data to prevent damage from extreme conditions.

Touch-Friendly UI

Optimized for outdoor and in-tractor use with large, clear touch targets.

Data Visualization

Simple, intuitive display of field and crop data for fast decision-making.

Design Process

01

Research & Understanding

Collaborated directly with farmers and agricultural experts, conducting interviews and field visits to understand real-world workflows, challenges, and needs. Collected insights on existing tools and practices to inform the design.

02

Concept & Ideation

Created low-fidelity sketches and wireframes to map key workflows. Defined the information hierarchy and interaction patterns for desktop and touch interfaces.

03

Design & Prototyping

Developed high-fidelity UI designs in Figma, including field mapping tools, irrigation controls, and data dashboards. Prototyped interactions to simulate touch gestures and real-world usage scenarios.

04

Collaboration & Iteration

Worked closely with developers and service designers to implement features and ensure cross-device usability. Conducted multiple rounds of usability testing, incorporating feedback to refine workflows and visuals.

05

Final Delivery

Produced a responsive web application with an intuitive interface, touch-optimized controls, and consistent visual identity. Documentation and design system provided for future scaling and feature updates.
