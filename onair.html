On Air

Cross platform concert streaming application

2018-2019

Overview

I designed the mobile experience for a streaming platform focused on live stage performances. My role covered the full design cycle — from early concepts and wireframes to final UI handoff and user testing. I worked closely with the art director, development team, and client stakeholders to ensure visual consistency and smooth functionality across platforms, maintaining a cohesive experience between mobile, web, and TV interfaces.

My Role

Design

Crafted the mobile UI/UX from early concepts to final interactive prototypes.

Adaptation

Translated the core experience seamlessly across iOS and Android platforms.

Collaboration

Worked closely with developers and the art director to ensure unified visuals and smooth implementation.

Validation

Conducted usability testing to refine content discovery and navigation.

Tools & Technologies

Sketch Adobe Creative Suite iOS Design System Android Material Design Prototyping

Key Features

Native Platform Design

Respected iOS and Android design guidelines while maintaining brand consistency across platforms.

Seamless Streaming

Intuitive video controls with gesture support for quick navigation and playback adjustments.

Smart Discovery

Personalized content recommendations with easy filtering and search capabilities.

Live Interaction

Real-time chat and reactions during live streams to enhance viewer engagement.

Impact

Created a cohesive mobile streaming experience across iOS and Android, bringing live stage performances to viewers' pockets. The intuitive interface made discovering and watching concerts effortless, while platform-specific adaptations ensured each version felt native. Helped establish OnAir's digital presence in the live entertainment streaming space.
