Design Process

My approach combines rigorous user research with rapid prototyping and validation. For each feature, I conduct discovery sessions including in-person CDS workshops and remote user interviews via Zoom. I use Maze to run surveys and gather quantitative data to inform design decisions.

Working in agile squads, I collaborate daily with Engineering Managers, Product Managers, and developers. I create high-fidelity prototypes in Figma and leverage the Instructure UI library to ensure design consistency. For the Block Editor redesign, I pioneered the use of Figma Make (vibe coding) to create functional prototypes that allowed us to test and validate complex interactions before engineering investment.

Throughout the design process, I work closely with accessibility engineers to ensure WCAG compliance. Every feature undergoes design validation sessions with stakeholders and users, ensuring we're building solutions that truly meet the needs of educators and students.

Team & Collaboration

I work in cross-functional agile squads consisting of an Engineering Manager, Product Manager, and myself as the Senior Product Designer, supported by a team of developers. This tight-knit structure enables rapid iteration and close collaboration throughout the product development cycle.

I regularly collaborate with other designers across Instructure, sharing insights and contributing to the Instructure UI library. Working with accessibility engineers is a core part of my process, ensuring that every design meets accessibility standards and serves all users effectively.
